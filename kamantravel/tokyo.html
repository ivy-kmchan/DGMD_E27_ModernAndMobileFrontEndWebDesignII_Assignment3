ABOUT TOKYO

Tokyo is the capital and the largest city of Japan. The city is super busy, and yet very beautiful and clean. The city is full of tall skyscrapers and the people in Tokyo are always in hustle and bustle. The streets are unbelieveable clean all the times. The Japanese food is good and the people there are nice. Tokyo is very fun to visit and you don't want to miss it!

TOKYO FOOD

Japanese food are not only tasty, but also look pretty. The traditional foods are rice, ramen, miso soup, fish, vegetable, etc. The sushi, with raw fish on top of the rice, is also very popular in Japan. Since Japan is an island nation which is surrounded by an ocean, it has abundant supply of fresh seafood. The typical seasonings include dashi, soy sauce, mirin, vinegar, sugar and salt. The Japapense cooking techniques are raw, grilled, simmered, steamed, deep-fried and vinegared. Japanese cuisine are beautiful and delicious!

TOKYO WEATHER

Tokyo has humid subtropical climate. It has fairly dry winters and warm and wet summers. Usually, the month August is the hottest month and the month of January is the coldest month. Snowfall is common in winter. The rainfall is seen in the months of March to October. In general, Tokyo has the moderate and comfortable climate year around.
